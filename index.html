Know before you throw

The House Rules.

01

Toss the Pallino.

Coin toss winner launches the little yellow ball. If it doesn't clear halfway down the lawn, the other team takes over. This is not a dramatic rule but it matters.

02

Throw underhand, from behind the line.

We are dignified professionals. Overhand tosses will be met with light judgment from coworkers.

03

Closest to the pallino wins the end.

1 point for every ball your team has closer than the opponent's nearest ball. 2 points for a "kiss" — your ball physically touching the pallino. Maximum drama, maximum points.

04

Hitting opponent balls is 100% encouraged.

Yes, really. Knock theirs out. Send them flying. The lawn is lawless within the lines.

05

First to 11. Must win by 2.

Tied at 10? You're going to 12. Tied at 12? Keep going. Someone has to lose; it might as well be your boss.
